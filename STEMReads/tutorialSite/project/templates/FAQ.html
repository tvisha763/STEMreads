What is the purpose of STEMreads?

STEMreads aims to provide a platform where students access articles created by both teachers and their peers on STEM related topics. This enhances students’ understanding of STEM while also offering practical advice and excelling in their STEM classes. By having a community of people with shared experiences, STEMreads allows students to engage deeply with STEM subjects and supports their academic success.

Who can write on STEMreads?

Everyone can apply to be a writer on STEMreads. Whether you’re a student, a teacher, or even a professional who has experience, STEMreads welcomes anyone to enhance the educational experience for readers.

Who can read on STEMreads?

Anyone can read on STEMreads, but STEMreads is targeted towards people who are mostly interested in STEM subjects. Whether you’re a student wanting to understand more about STEM, a teacher looking for more educational resources, or someone that just likes STEM, STEMreads is for you.

Why should I apply to be a writer?

Writing for STEMreads can lead to an engaging experience for people who are passionate about STEM. It can enhance your knowledge and writing skills while also providing a platform to share your views with anyone. By helping STEMreads, it can help you develop communication skills and also gain recognition for your own work in the community!

Why should I read articles on STEMreads?

STEMreads articles offer students to engage with their peers and teachers on STEM topics while also learning from their classes. This interactive platform allows a collaborative environment where students can deepen their understanding and share perspectives with one another to help with their STEM education.
