Formatting

Always format your article in markdown.
Markdown cheat sheet: https://www.markdownguide.org/cheat-sheet/
You can use markdown to bold, italicize, highlight, add headings, quotes, lists, tables, etc. I encourage you to use these tools to make your articles more engaging and easy to read.
Embedding a Youtube video to enhance your article is strongly suggested. You can film a video of your own, or you can just use one you find on Youtube that fits the topic of your article. To find the embed code of a video, right click on the video and select "copy embed code". You can now embed this video into your article simply pasting it into the video embed textbox.
Please try to credit any sources used for research.
